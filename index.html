Frequently Asked Questions

How are excitement scores calculated?
I analyze every game using 20+ factors including final margin, lead changes, clutch plays, overtime, game flow, pace, and scoring patterns. Each factor is weighted based on its impact on game excitement, then combined into a 0-10 score. Higher scores mean more exciting games!
When is data updated?
Data is updated every morning around 6 AM CET with analysis of yesterday's games.
