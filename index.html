Frequently Asked Questions

How are excitement scores calculated?
We analyze every game using 20+ factors including final margin, lead changes, clutch plays, overtime, game flow, pace, and scoring patterns. Each factor is weighted based on its impact on game excitement, then combined into a 0-10 score. Higher scores mean more exciting games!
When is data updated?
Data is updated every morning around 6 AM CET with analysis of yesterday's games. During heavy game nights, updates may take slightly longer as we process all the data.
What do the labels mean?
Down to the Wire: Game decided in final seconds
Scoring Explosion: High-scoring offensive showcase
Pickpocket Alert: Defensive battle with lots of steals
Chaos Ball: Wild, unpredictable back-and-forth
Easy Win: One-sided game, not worth your time
Blowout Alert: 30+ point margin
Is this app affiliated with the NBA?
No, Heatcheck is an independent app created by a fan. We use publicly available data from NBA.com and link to official NBA highlight videos. We're not endorsed by or affiliated with the NBA.
Why is the app free?
Built by a solo developer as a passion project to solve my own problem as a new dad and European NBA fan. There's an optional "Buy me a Gatorade" support option, but the core app is and will remain free.
What's coming next? ROADMAP
Coming soon: Push notifications for exciting games, team filters, historical data, player-based labels, advanced analytics, and more! Follow us on Twitter for updates.

About Heatcheck

Heatcheck was created by a European NBA fan who became a dad and suddenly had limited time to follow basketball. Instead of wasting precious morning minutes trying to figure out which games were worth watching, I built an app to solve the problem.

What started as a simple script evolved into a full passion project. Now it helps fans around the world (especially in Europe) find the most exciting games without spoilers.

Solo developer. Real problem. Simple solution.
